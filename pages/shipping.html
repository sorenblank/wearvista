Shipping and Return Policy

Welcome to WearVista!

Shipping

At WearVista, we are committed to delivering your orders promptly and efficiently. Please review our shipping policy to understand how we handle the shipment of your purchases:

1. Processing Time: Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.

2. Shipping Methods: We offer various shipping methods to meet your needs, including standard and expedited options. The available methods and estimated delivery times will be displayed during the checkout process.

3. Shipping Costs: Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight, size, and destination of your order. You can view the shipping charges during the checkout process before finalizing your purchase.

4. Delivery: We strive to deliver your orders within the estimated delivery times. However, please note that actual delivery times may vary depending on factors such as the shipping method chosen and your location.

Returns

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from WearVista. If you are not satisfied with your order, please read our return policy for details on how to initiate a return:

1. Eligibility: To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in its original condition, and in the original packaging. Returns are typically accepted within 30 days of the purchase date.

2. Initiating a Return: To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team at [customer support email] within 30 days of receiving your order. Provide your order number and a brief explanation of the reason for the return.

3. Return Shipping: You will be responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the return is due to a mistake on our part. We recommend using a trackable shipping service to ensure that we receive your return.

4. Refund Process: Once we receive your returned item and inspect it, we will process your refund. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Please allow [processing time] for the refund to be processed and reflected in your account.

5. Exchanges: If you wish to exchange an item, please contact our customer support team, and they will assist you in the exchange process.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding our shipping and return policy, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team at [customer support email]. We are here to help you!
